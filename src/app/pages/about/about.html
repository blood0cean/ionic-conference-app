Worth Academy Day

Date Location Netherlands, Zuid-Holland
An action-packed day for all Worthies to enjoy, be inspired, share knowledge and make cool stuff!

It’s nearly Worth Academy Day! What does that mean for us at Worth? It means that our NL headquarters will once again transform into a bustling campus to stage our very own Worth Academy Day! This is an action-packed day for all Worthies to enjoy, be inspired, share knowledge, and experiment with technology.

Following in the footsteps of the inaugural 2017 Academy Day, we'll be taking in:

  • Lectures and presentations
  • Workshops
  • Demonstrations
  • Tinkering in the Worth Lab, experimenting with IoT
  • And much, much more!
