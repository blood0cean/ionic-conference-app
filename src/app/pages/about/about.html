It’s nearly Worth Academy Day! What does that mean for us at Worth? It means that our online stream for the first time will into a bustling campus to stage our very own Worth Academy Day! This is an action-packed day for all Worthies to enjoy, be inspired, share knowledge, and experiment with technology.

Following in the footsteps of the inaugural 2017 Academy Day, we'll be taking in:

  • Lectures and presentations
  • Workshops
  • Demonstrations
  • Tinkering in the Worth Lab, experimenting with IoT
  • And much, much more!
